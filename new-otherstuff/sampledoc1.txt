Pizza recipe:
Bloom yeast in 2 cups warm water, 1 tbs sugar
add olive 2 tbs and salt 1 tbs and mix
add 5 cups ap flour or bread flour and knead till good gluten development
rise for ~ 2h
stretch into pizza, add pizza sauce, mozzarella cheese 
heat pizza steel for min 30min 
shimmy pizza onto steel with semolina flour
remove
